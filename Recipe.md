# **Chocolate Brownie**
## __Ingredients__
1. Chocolate
2. Eggs
3. Milk
4. Butter
5. All Purpose Flour

## __Preparation__
1. Melt the butter and chocolate
2. let the mixture cool
3. Add the eggs and milk
4. Mix
5. Add the all purpose flour and mix
6. Bake for 20 minutes at 180 celcius
7. let it completely cool and cut into 12 pieces
